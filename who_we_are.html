Who we are?

At ConquerBlocks ACADEMY, we are a community of educators, developers, and technology enthusiasts who share a common mission: to turn learning into a practical, fun, and transformative experience. We believe that anyone can learn to code with clear guidance, real-world projects, and the support of a community that encourages steady, step-by-step progress. That’s why we design each course with a progressive methodology: first we understand, then we practice, and finally we create.
Our team combines academic and professional experience to deliver up-to-date content in areas such as web development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and automation, helping students build skills that are truly relevant in today’s world.

Our values

  • Learning by doing: each lesson includes real-world exercises or projects.
  • Community:we believe knowledge grows when it is shared.
  • Continuous innovation: we update our content every academic cycle
  • Accessibility: technology education for everyone, without barriers.

Our history

CONQUERblocks ACADEMY was founded in 2022 as an independent initiative by a group of programmers who were looking for a more practical and approachable way to teach technology. We started by sharing free resources and soon grew into an international online learning community. Today, we remain faithful to that purpose: teaching programming from scratch with a human, collaborative, and future-focused approach.
